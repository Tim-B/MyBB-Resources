MyBB 1.2.3 was released on 14 February 2007 to fix reported bugs and address low-risk security vulnerabilities

Happy Valentines Day!

And on this day full of love and happiness we're releasing an update to the MyBB 1.2 series. MyBB 1.2.3 is a general maintenance release as well as a security update release that patches two recently discovered low-medium threat vulnerabilities.

Over 80 bugs were identified with MyBB 1.2.2 through bug report submissions, beta testing and internal analysis. These bugs have been fixed to provide a more stable version of MyBB for public use.

We recommend that all users upgrade to 1.2.3 so their board is patched against these discovered vulnerabilities and are running a more stable version of the MyBB 1.2 series.

More information here: http://community.mybb.com/showthread.php?tid=16273
