On April 1st, 2011 chaos struck the MyBB Community forums, everything was..... Pirate?

The staff decided, nothing wrong with a joke and changed the language pack on the community forum to a specially made pirate language pack (made by Tomm). Everyone saw everything in pirate language... Nothing was the same!

The pirate pack lasted over 48hours to allow everyone in every timezone to get pranked. In addition, after popular demand, Tomm released his language pack as a public download, allowing anyone to change their forums language.

You can read about this Aprils Fools Day prank here: http://community.mybb.com/thread-91327.html You can download Tomm's Pirate language pack here: http://community.mybb.com/thread-91584.html
